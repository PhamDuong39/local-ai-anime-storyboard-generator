## Goal

Do not ask me for a specific product.
Use live web search. Do not rely only on assumptions.

Before filling Product Link or FB Ads Competitor link, actively search the web.

For every product candidate, try to find:
- one product/supplier link from Amazon, Alibaba, AliExpress, Walmart, Shopify store, or another accessible source

- one Meta/Facebook Ads Library link if publicly accessible

Only write "Need research" after attempting live search.

Start from the category, generate US English seed keywords, search/reason through competitor products, filter weak products, and return the best product candidates.

## Required Workflow

Follow this process:

1. Convert my category into US English seed keywords.
2. Search or identify relevant products from:
   - Meta/Facebook Ads Library
   - Google US
   - Amazon US
   - Walmart
   - TikTok/TikTok Shop
   - AliExpress or supplier platforms
   - Shopify competitor stores
3. Find competitor brands when possible.
4. Check whether similar products are already sold cheaply in the US.
5. Filter using the mandatory product criteria:
   - Solves a big problem or strong desire
   - COGS/Revenue likely below 35%
   - AOV ideally $40 - $100
   - Has pattern interruption or strong visual demo
   - Low or manageable saturation
   - Clear differentiation angle
   - Small and lightweight
   - Safe and compliant
   - Product likely works and has good review potential
   - Trend is rising or stable
   - Easy for me to create ad content
6. Select the best 5 product candidates.
7. Fill the output table.
